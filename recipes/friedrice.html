Egg fried rice

Description

This recipe teaches you how to make egg fried rice

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Eggs
  • Sunflower oil
  • Light soy sauce
  • Dark soy sauce
  • MSG
  • Spring onion

Steps

  1. Cook rice and wait at least 2 hours for it to cool if using microwave rice ignore this step
  2. Chop the spring onion
  3. Fry the spring onion
  4. When the spring onion is mostly cooked add and scramble the eggs
  5. Before the eggs completely set add 2tsp of light soy and a pinch of MSG
  6. Add the rice to the pan and mix well with the egg
  7. Add 1tbsp of light and dark soy sauce as well as a pinch of MSG
  8. Cook until the rice is hot then server
