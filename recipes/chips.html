Chips

Description

This recipe teaches you how to make chips easily in an air fryer

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Vegetable oil or sunflower oil
  • Salt
  • Malt vinegar

Steps

  1. Peel and chop potatoes into chips
  2. Salt and boil a pan of water
  3. Add 1 tbsp of vinegar to the water
  4. Add potatoes and simmer for 5 minutes
  5. Drain water then coat the potatoes in oil
  6. Cook potatoes in air fryer for 20 minutes at 200c remove halfway through cooking to toss then
